## Free Tier Strategy

### What Makes Our Free Tier Better:

1. **Actually Useful Limits**
   - Not "5 files then pay"
   - "50 files/day" = 1,500/month free
   - Real users can actually work with this

2. **No Watermarks**
   - Competitors brand free outputs
   - We don't (builds goodwill)

3. **No Time Limits**
   - Free forever, not trial
   - Builds long-term user base

4. **Full Feature Access**
   - Not "basic only"
   - All features, just limited volume
