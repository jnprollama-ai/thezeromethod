## Customer Testimonial Policy

**For REAL testimonials only:**

1. Customer must give explicit written permission
2. We verify they actually bought the product
3. We keep the raw feedback (don't edit to make it better)
4. We include their real name and title (or anonymous if they prefer)
5. We also include constructive criticism

**Template for collecting:**
```
Hi [Name],

Would you be willing to share your experience with Zero Method?

We're looking for honest feedback - what worked, what didn't, how much time you saved (or didn't).

You can remain anonymous if you prefer.

Thanks!
- Zero
```

## Authenticity Checklist

Before publishing any post:

- [ ] Is this something we actually tested/used?
- [ ] Are the results real?
- [ ] Did we include setbacks/limitations?
- [ ] Is the tone honest (not hype-y)?
- [ ] Would we send this to a skeptical friend?
- [ ] No fake quotes or testimonials?
- [ ] No inflated numbers?

**If not, don't publish.**
