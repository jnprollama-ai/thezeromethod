## Step 2: Registrar Setup

**Recommended: Namecheap**
- URL: https://www.namecheap.com
- Price: ~$10-13/year for .com
- Includes: Free WHOIS privacy (hides your info)
- Features: Easy DNS management, email forwarding

**Alternative: Cloudflare Registrar**
- URL: https://www.cloudflare.com/products/registrar/
- Price: Wholesale cost (~$9/year for .com)
- Best for: If you plan to use Cloudflare for CDN/security later
- Note: Requires Cloudflare account

---

## Step 3: Purchase Process

### For Namecheap:

1. **Go to** https://www.namecheap.com
2. **Search for:** `zeromethod.com`
3. **Check availability**
4. **If available:**
   - Click "Add to Cart"
   - Domain Privacy: **Enabled** (free)
   - PremiumDNS: **Skip** (not needed)
   - Email: **Skip** (we'll handle this separately)
5. **Create account** (if new)
6. **Checkout** with your payment method
7. **Verify email** from Namecheap

### For Cloudflare:

1. **Go to** https://dash.cloudflare.com
2. **Sign up / Log in**
3. **Add site:** Enter domain
4. **Select plan:** Free
5. **Go to Registrar section**
6. **Search** `zeromethod.com`
7. **Purchase** if available
