## CRM Strategy: Zero Lead System

### Purpose:
Track, nurture, and convert prospects into buyers

### Architecture:

**Stage 1: Awareness**
- Content consumers (blog readers, social followers)
- Email subscribers (free prompt guide)
- Tracking: Source, engagement level, topics of interest

**Stage 2: Interest**
- Downloaded lead magnet
- Engaged with multiple emails
- Visited sales page
- Tracking: Email opens, link clicks, page visits

**Stage 3: Consideration**
- Pricing page visits
- Email replies/questions
- Social engagement on product content
- Tracking: Heatmap data, time on site

**Stage 4: Purchase**
- Bought product
- Tracking: Revenue, tier purchased, upsell potential

**Stage 5: Advocacy**
- Testimonials provided
- Referrals made
- Repeat purchases
- Tracking: Lifetime value, referral rate

### CRM Tool Selection:

**Option A: Notion (Free)**
- Custom database
- Kanban view for pipeline
- Linked records for contacts/deals
- Good for visual management

**Option B: Airtable (Free tier)**
- More powerful automations
- Form submissions
- Email integration potential
- Scales better

**Option C: Google Sheets + Automation (Free)**
- Simple but effective
- Easy to export/share
- Can trigger webhooks
- Most flexible

**Recommendation:** Start with **Airtable** — professional, scalable, free tier generous.

### CRM Data Structure:

**Contact Record:**
- Name, email, source
- Acquisition date
- Lead score (engagement-based)
- Stage (awareness → interest → consideration → purchase → advocacy)
- Tags (persona, pain point, interest area)
- Last touch date
- Next action required

**Deal Record:**
- Contact (linked)
- Product tier
- Value
- Stage
- Probability
- Expected close date
- Notes

### Lead Nurturing Flow:

**Day 0:** Welcome + free prompt guide
**Day 2:** Case study (someone who saved 10 hours)
**Day 4:** Behind the scenes (how prompts are built)
**Day 6:** Social proof (testimonials)
**Day 8:** FAQ (address objections)
**Day 10:** Limited-time bonus offer
**Day 14:** Final CTA
**Day 21:** Value-add content (unrelated to sale)
**Ongoing:** Weekly tips newsletter
