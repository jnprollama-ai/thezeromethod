Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. What We Collect

We collect only what's necessary:

  • Email address — For order confirmation and product updates
  • Name — Optional, for personalization
  • Payment info — Processed securely by Stripe (we never see your card details)

2. What We Don't Collect

We don't:

  • Track you across the web
  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Use creepy retargeting ads
  • Store your payment details (Stripe handles that)

3. How We Use Your Data

Your email is used for:

  • Sending your purchase confirmation
  • Product updates (only if you bought something)
  • Support responses (when you contact us)

That's it. No marketing spam. No sharing with partners.

4. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Unsubscribe from emails anytime

Just email support@thezeromethod.com

5. Security

We use industry-standard security:

  • HTTPS everywhere
  • Stripe for payments (PCI compliant)
  • No plaintext storage of sensitive data

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Essential site functionality only
  • No tracking cookies
  • No advertising cookies

7. Changes

If we change this policy, we'll email you. We won't sneak in changes.

8. Contact

Questions? support@thezeromethod.com
