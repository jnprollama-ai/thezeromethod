# Zero Marketing Agency Research Notes

**Focus**: Competitor Analysis & Content Gap Identification

## Competitor Analysis Summary

### Major Players
1. **PromptHero** (https://www.prompthero.com)
   - Focus: Creative prompt marketplace
   - Pricing: $19-$49 subscription model
   - Content: Video tutorials, community prompts
   - Strengths: Strong visual branding, active community
   - Weaknesses: No beginner guides, assumes technical knowledge

2. **FlowGPT** (https://flowgpt.com)
   - Focus: Workflow-based prompt sharing
   - Pricing: Free + $10/month premium
   - Content: User-submitted prompts organized by workflow
   - Strengths: Large prompt database, categorization system
   - Weaknesses: Poor quality control, no educational content

3. **LearnPrompting.org** (https://learnprompting.org)
   - Focus: Educational prompt engineering course
   - Pricing: Free online book + paid certifications
   - Content: Structured curriculum, exercises, examples
   - Strengths: Comprehensive educational approach
   - Weaknesses: Too technical for business professionals

### Content Gap Identified
**Missing**: Beginner-friendly prompt engineering guides specifically for non-technical professionals who want immediate business applications.

Most existing resources either:
- Assume technical/developer background
- Focus on creative/artistic uses
- Are too abstract without concrete business examples

### Opportunity for Zero Marketing Agency
Position our content as:
"Prompt Engineering for Professionals Who Don't Have Time to Learn AI But Need Results"

Specific angles to explore:
1. "5 Prompts That Replace an Assistant" 
2. "How to Get Better Meeting Notes with AI"
3. "Automate Report Writing with These 3 Prompts"
4. "Customer Service Response Templates Using AI"
