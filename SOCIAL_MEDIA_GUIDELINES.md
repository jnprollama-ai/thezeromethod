## 🚨 CRITICAL RULES - NEVER VIOLATE

### 1. **Twitter Terms of Service**
**Automated Actions Limits (Free Tier):**
- ✅ Max 50 tweets/day (well under 1,500/month limit)
- ✅ Max 1,000 likes/day
- ✅ Max 500 retweets/day
- ✅ Max 2,400 follows/day (but we limit to 20-30/day)
- ❌ NEVER automate DMs to non-followers (spam violation)
- ❌ NEVER use multiple accounts to game engagement
- ❌ NEVER buy followers or engagement

**Our Limits (Conservative):**
- 3-5 tweets/day maximum
- 20-30 targeted follows/day
- 50-100 likes/day (thoughtful, not random)
- 10-20 meaningful replies/day

---

### 2. **Content Guidelines**

**✅ APPROVED Content:**
- Original insights about AI/productivity
- Helpful tips and tutorials
- Genuine responses to questions
- Sharing valuable resources
- Authentic engagement with community

**❌ PROHIBITED Content:**
- Misinformation about AI capabilities
- Unsubstantiated claims ("get rich quick")
- Copyrighted material without attribution
- Hate speech, harassment, discrimination
- Explicit or NSFW content
- Political content (unless directly relevant to business)
- Spam: repetitive posts, excessive hashtags
- Impersonation or fake endorsements

---

### 3. **Authentic Engagement Rules**

**DO:**
- Read tweets before liking/replying
- Reply with substance, not just emojis
- Follow accounts relevant to your niche
- Engage with genuine interest
- Provide value in every interaction
- Space out activities naturally
- Be transparent about automation (in bio)

**DON'T:**
- Like tweets without reading
- Post identical replies to multiple people
- Follow-unfollow rapidly (aggressive growth hack)
- Use engagement pods or groups
- Post only promotional content
- Tag people who don't know you without context
- Use excessive hashtags (max 2-3 per tweet)

---

### 4. **Best Practices Checklist**

**Before Every Tweet:**
- [ ] Is this providing value?
- [ ] Is it relevant to our audience?
- [ ] Is the tone respectful and professional?
- [ ] Are claims accurate and substantiated?
- [ ] Would I say this in person?
- [ ] Is the hashtag relevant (not trending abuse)?

**Before Every Follow:**
- [ ] Is this person in our target audience?
- [ ] Do they post relevant content?
- [ ] Are they active (not dormant accounts)?
- [ ] Is their content appropriate?

**Before Every Reply:**
- [ ] Did I read the original tweet?
- [ ] Is my reply helpful or insightful?
- [ ] Am I adding to the conversation?
- [ ] Is the tone appropriate?

---

### 5. **Respect Rate Limits**

**Our Conservative Limits:**
```
Tweets: Max 3-5 per day (space 3-4 hours apart)
Likes: Max 50-100 per day (not all at once)
Replies: Max 10-20 per day (thoughtful only)
Follows: Max 20-30 per day (relevant accounts only)
Retweets: Max 5-10 per day (quality over quantity)
```

**Timing:**
- Space all actions by at least 30-60 seconds
- Never rapid-fire actions
- Vary timing to appear natural
- Peak hours: 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM, 7-9 PM (your timezone)

---

### 6. **Transparency Requirements**

**Bio Should Include:**
- "AI-powered account" or "Automated tweets"
- Or: "Managed by @ZeroMethodAI"
- Disclosure builds trust

**Avoid:**
- Pretending to be human when fully automated
- Not disclosing automated content
- Fake testimonials or endorsements

---

### 7. **Community Etiquette**

**Be a Good Citizen:**
- Credit sources when sharing others' content
- Don't hijack threads with self-promotion
- Respect people's time (keep threads focused)
- Don't argue or troll
- Block/mute don't engage with negativity
- Support others in your niche

**Thread Etiquette:**
- 1/5, 2/5, etc. format is fine
- Space tweets 30-60 seconds apart minimum
- Don't post more than 1 thread/day
- Make each tweet standalone (in case of engagement)

---

### 8. **Content Quality Standards**

**Every Tweet Must:**
1. Provide educational value OR
2. Share actionable insight OR
3. Ask thoughtful question OR
4. Share relevant news with context

**Never Post:**
- "Check out my product" without value first
- Generic motivational quotes
- Low-effort content
- Clickbait headlines
- Engagement bait ("RT if you agree")

---

### 9. **Crisis Management**

**If Mistake Happens:**
1. Delete offending content immediately
2. Acknowledge error if necessary
3. Learn and adjust automation
4. Review guidelines
5. Continue with improved practices

**Red Lines:**
- If account gets warned: Pause automation immediately
- If rate limited: Wait 24 hours before resuming
- If suspended: Appeal and document what went wrong

---

### 10. **Monitoring & Compliance**

**Daily Checks:**
- Review all automated tweets before posting
- Check for any community feedback/issues
- Monitor engagement quality vs. quantity
- Ensure no policy violations

**Weekly Reviews:**
- Analyze which content performed best
- Adjust strategy based on feedback
- Review followed accounts (unfollow inactive/spam)
- Check for any Twitter policy updates
