## Step 2: Build Your Knowledge Base (1 hour)

Your AI assistant needs to know YOU.

### Create a "About Me" document with:

**Professional Info:**
- Your role and industry
- Your company/services
- Your target audience
- Your unique value proposition
- Common questions you answer

**Communication Style:**
- Tone (professional, casual, witty)
- Words you use often
- Words you avoid
- Formatting preferences

**Content Examples:**
- Your best performing emails
- Your most popular social posts
- Articles you've written
- Testimonials from clients

**Templates:**
- Email response templates
- Meeting request templates
- Proposal structures
- Content outlines

**Save this in Notion or as a document you can upload to ChatGPT.**

---

## Step 3: Create Your Custom GPT (15 minutes)

### In ChatGPT Plus:

1. Click "Explore GPTs"
2. Click "Create"
3. Configure your assistant:

**Name:** Your Personal Assistant

**Description:** An AI assistant trained on my communication style, business context, and preferences.

**Instructions:**
```
You are my personal AI assistant. Here's what you need to know:

[Copy your "About Me" document here]

When helping me:
1. Match my communication style
2. Reference my templates when appropriate
3. Ask clarifying questions if needed
4. Provide 2-3 options when possible

Tone: [YOUR PREFERRED TONE]
Style: [YOUR STYLE PREFERENCES]
```

**Knowledge:** Upload your documents

**Save and start using!**

---

## Step 4: Set Up Automations (30 minutes)

### Use Zapier to connect your tools:

**Workflow 1: Email Assistant**
```
Trigger: New email in Gmail
Action: Create draft in Gmail using ChatGPT

Prompt: "Draft a response to this email: [email content]. Use my usual style and include a call-to-action."
```

**Workflow 2: Content Creator**
```
Trigger: New row in Google Sheets
Action: Generate social post in ChatGPT

Prompt: "Create a [platform] post about [topic]. Include a hook and call-to-action."
```

**Workflow 3: Meeting Prep**
```
Trigger: Calendar event starting in 15 minutes
Action: Create document in Notion

Prompt: "Research [meeting topic] and create a briefing with key points to discuss."
```

---

## Step 5: Train Your Assistant (Ongoing)

### The Feedback Loop:

**When you like the output:**
- Save it as an example
- Tell AI "This is exactly what I wanted"
- Add to your knowledge base

**When you don't like the output:**
- Explain what was wrong
- Provide a corrected version
- Update instructions

**Pro Tip:** Keep a "Wins" document with your best AI outputs. Reference these as examples.

## Common Questions

**Q: Will it really sound like me?**
A: Yes, if you provide enough examples and iterate. Start with 5-10 examples of your best work.

**Q: Can it handle complex tasks?**
A: Break complex tasks into steps. AI is great at each individual step.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Don't upload sensitive personal data. Use AI for professional tasks only.

**Q: What if I don't have ChatGPT Plus?**
A: You can use the free version, but you won't be able to create custom GPTs. Use detailed prompts instead.
