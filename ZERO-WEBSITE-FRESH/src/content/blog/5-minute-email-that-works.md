# The 5-Minute Email That Actually Works

Most emails fail before the first word is written.

The sender doesn't know what they want. The recipient can't tell what's being asked. Everyone wastes time.

Here's a framework that fixes it.

## The 3-Part Structure

Every email needs exactly three things:

### 1. Context (1 sentence)
*Why are you writing?*

> "Following up on our meeting yesterday..."

### 2. Request (1 sentence)
*What do you need?*

> "I need your approval on the Q2 budget by Friday."

### 3. Action (1 sentence)
*What should they do?*

> "Reply with yes/no, or call me if you need clarification."

## Why It Works

- **Respects time**: Busy people can scan and decide in 10 seconds
- **Reduces friction**: Clear requests get clear responses
- **Forces clarity**: If you can't fit it in 3 sentences, you don't know what you want

## The Template

```
Hi [Name],

[Context]. [Request]. [Action].

Thanks,
[Your name]
```

Try it. Your response rate will improve immediately.
