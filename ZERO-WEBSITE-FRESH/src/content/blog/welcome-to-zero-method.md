# Welcome to Zero Method

Productivity advice is broken.

Most systems ask you to do *more*. More apps. More habits. More optimization.

We think that's backwards.

## The Problem with Modern Productivity

- **Tool overload**: 47 apps to manage your life
- **Optimization obsession**: Spending hours tweaking systems instead of doing work
- **Complexity creep**: Every solution creates new problems

## Our Approach

**Zero Method** is about subtraction, not addition.

1. **Identify what actually matters**
2. **Remove everything else**
3. **Execute with ruthless simplicity**

It's not about being busy. It's about being effective.

## What to Expect

This blog cuts through the noise. No 5,000-word guides. No complex frameworks. Just practical insights you can apply immediately.

Let's build something better.
